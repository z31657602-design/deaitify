Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Data Collection

DeAItify processes text locally in your browser. No text content is uploaded to our servers without your knowledge. We do not store, share, or sell your text data.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics (such as feature usage counts) to improve our service. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for service functionality (e.g., free trial counts, session management). No third-party tracking cookies are used without your consent.

AI Processing Transparency

We utilize artificial intelligence algorithms to rewrite text. Your input text is processed by our AI with end-to-end encryption and is not stored or used for model training. All AI processing is performed in real-time and discarded immediately after the session.

Third-Party Services

We use industry-standard AI APIs (MiniMax) solely for text processing. These services process your text temporarily and do not store it beyond the session.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.
