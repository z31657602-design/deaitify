# DeAItify

Make AI text sound human. Free AI humanizer for academic writing.

**Website:** https://laolang.fun

## Overview

DeAItify is a free tool that helps students, researchers, and content creators humanize AI-generated text. Our mission is to make AI writing sound natural and authentic.

## Features

- **AI Humanizer:** Lower AI detection scores without losing meaning
- **Academic Writing:** Perfect for thesis, research papers, and academic documents
- **100% Free:** No hidden costs, no signup required
