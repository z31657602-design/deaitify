# About DeAItify

DeAItify (laolang.fun) is a free AI text humanization tool designed for academic writers, researchers, and graduate students. Our rewriting engine targets the structural patterns that AI detection tools flag -- helping ensure human-written work is not incorrectly penalized.

**Try it free at:** [laolang.fun](https://laolang.fun)
